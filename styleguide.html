3. Corner radius

One ladder for every rounded corner, mirroring the Tailwind/Radix convention. Controls (buttons, inputs, chips, badges) use sm; cards and list rows use md; the outer page panels use lg. The old fully-round "pill" shapes are gone - full is now reserved only for things that are genuinely circular (status dots, the live-workout progress track, slider thumbs).
